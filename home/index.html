                   Started way back in 2020. Hostelwala website is used to find the student affordable hostels near Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College. This website is started with a vision to provide all the hostelers to chose their home away from home according to their taste. As students have no idea about facilities provided by the hostels, here we integrated all the facilities and reviews directly from the students. The core theme of the hostelwala has been to help the hostellers in finding safe, hygenic, securely located and affordable hostels.
